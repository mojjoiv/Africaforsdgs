SDG MTAANI/ TALK SDGS

SDG Mtaani program is focusing on creating awareness on Sustainable Development Goals in communities in Africa starting with Kenya. It helps young people engage with different stakeholders in engaging issues affecting them through focused discussions, project formation and implementation and policy formulation. The purpose of this program is to present a multi sectoral dialogue on SDGs at the local level dubbed The TalkSDGs series, a Focused Group Discussion forum which brings together youth and change makers from all works of life to rethink and rework on methodologies for bringing the messages of the SDGs to the public. This methodology will also provide a platform to show case to decision makers the positive actions by young people and development partners at the local, national and regional levels for inspiration and synergy around the implementation of the SDGs. This concept will explore the areas around awareness creation, taking action and holding the relevant stakeholders accountable on the progress or lack thereof. TalkSDGs Series will focus on topics that encourage active engagements by the local change makers who are either interested or already making progress in such topics in their personal, professional or academic lives in a moderated discussion that is open to the public. Discussions will inform action on the existing gaps in the implementation of SDGs, whose responsibility, what solutions to the gaps, role of stakeholders and finally come up with action plan for action by the stakeholders. Target group: Youth in Communities, Youth leaders.
