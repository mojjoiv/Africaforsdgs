Our Organisation

Africa For SDGs is an initiative under My Goals Foundation that seeks to promote youth participation in the Sustainable Development Goals in Africa by mobilizing and providing a platform to participate in these universal goals in order to meet urgent social, economic and environmental challenges facing Africa. This is by creating awareness and coming up with innovative, implementable and scalable solutions that can be implemented at their local communities. Our unique role is to provide knowledge, skills and attitude that will encourage innovation and facilitate partnership with various stakeholders and communities for implementation. The initiative promotes youth mobilizing and organizing for participation in practical ways that contributes towards eradication of poverty and all forms of inequality while promoting the protection of the environment. It acknowledges the youth bulge and seeks to ensure that it does not undermine economic growth, peace and security in Africa. The initiative provides a framework that pursues a multisectoral approaches which establishes coordination mechanisms to combine the efforts of development agencies, corporate sectors and government departments across ministries to enhance youth skills and opportunities aligned to the SDGs.

    • Our Mission

    Creating Awareness among the youth on what SDGs are and how they can be involved in implementation, monitoring and accountability process. Building Youth capacity to learn and research on sdgs in order to engage effectively in global affairs locally through training for knowledge skills and attitude transformation for implementation of SDGs. To create a platform for the youth to actively engage in the implementation process of the SDGs through active citizenship community engagement and public action and innovation.

  • Environmental Restoration

    • Our Vision

    The youth would make a deliberate decision to engage with the goals, create awareness and participate to address urgent social economic and environmental issues facing Africa. The youth would come together build strong partnerships and alliances and collaborate in realizing as well as monitoring the goals while ensuring accountability.

  • Environmental Restoration

    • Our Theory Of Change

    The youth would share their experiences, lessons, challenges and success in realizing these goals. We believe that when youths are capacitated with leadership and development skills, knowledge and attitude, then they become active citizens in their respective communities and countries. This encourages young people’s participation, enlisting them in designing and implementing policies, programs, and services that are directed at them and their communities. Youth involvement ensures that policy actions are relevant and helps develop youth as partners and leaders in development. This approach asserts the importance of viewing the youth as assets and active agents of change, who can contribute their energy, idealism, and insights to a nation’s growth and progress; and in the end the SDGs. The program seeks to involve the youth in reviewing Global indicators, developing innovative enterprises that are implementable and scalable local approaches towards sustainable development and develop program partnerships and collaborations to increase youth participation and philanthropy, develop stronger public and private partnerships, increase active citizenship through community engagements and leadership. In the end, we intend to see increased youth engagement and youth leadership in formal and informal policy processes, better policy designs and implementation, increased social capital for realization of sustainable development, improved public private partnership for efficient service delivery, increased patriotism and civic participation.
