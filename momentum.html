Generation Momentum

Creating Caring, Responsible and Innovative Young Leaders As our world becomes smaller through globalization and advancing technology, parents and educators world over share concerns about raising the younger generation. Family breakdown, sexually immorality,substance abuse, environmental degradation, media influence and youths challenging of authority and tradition are just a few of the wide spread concerns. As traditional religious, civic, and family values come into question in our rapidly changing world, adults sometimes feel they are swimming upstream in a culture saturated with images of violence, sexual incense, and material ease.Character Education, Leadership Development Program and a Mentorship program dubbed ‘Generation Momentum’. The aim is to create schools that foster ethical, responsible, caring and innovative young people through modelling and teaching good character and creativity through an emphasis on universal values that we all share. This is not a “quick-fix” or silver-bullet cure-all. It provides long-term solutions that address moral, ethical and academic issues that are a growing concern in our society and the safety of our schools. Generation Momentum is a deliberate effort to help young people understand, care about, and acts upon core ethical values. More importantly, it provides students with knowledge, skills,virtues, and confidence to actively participate in community life in consonance with Social Development Goals. The program has a Service-Learning component which connects meaningful service experienceswith academic learning, personal growth and civic responsibility.

  • Environmental Restoration

    • Objective

    • overall objective
    The program focuses on the development of the ‘Whole person’, namely; Cognitive, Affective and Behavioural in relation to three basic life goals.
    • To become fully mature and responsible individual
    • To develop loving relationships, especially in a family
    • To make a positive contribution to society through creativity and mastery
    The concept of life goals provides a larger perspective for conceptualizing character, peace and leadership development. Appealing to youth’s imagination with attractive image of growth, relationships, and creativity, a life goal framework allows teachers to fully articulate the ‘WHY’ of good behaviour.

    • Specific Objectives

    • To enhance performance and time management while at school
    • To build self-Discipline, responsibility and awareness on own abilities and talents

    • To Promote Community service among school-going youth
